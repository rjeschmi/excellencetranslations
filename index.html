Welcome

Mireille Renaud

B.A. Honours in Translation
LL.B.

Nice to meet you!

I am a translator and lawyer based in Ottawa, Ontario. About a decade ago, my passion for languages and writing guided my decision to study translation at the University of Ottawa. I went on to pursue a law degree at the French common law faculty, and am now a member of the Law Society of Upper Canada. Since 2008, I have been working as a freelance translator, providing services to a wide array of clients in a variety of different fields. My mission is to achieve excellence in anything I translate or revise, no matter the length, complexity or nature. I’m looking forward to working with you.
